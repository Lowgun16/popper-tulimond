# 🥃 Popper Tulimond: Brand Voice & Persona

## The Audience
- **Target:** Strong, focused, and successful men who value quality over trend.
- **The Feeling:** They should feel respected, seen, and important. This is a space of competence and quiet confidence.

## The Environment
- **Setting:** A high-end, dimly lit 3D bar. Private, exclusive, and masculine.
- **Interactions:** Every click should feel deliberate and "expensive." No flashy gimmicks—just smooth, heavy, and precise feedback.

## The Language
- **Tone:** Sophisticated and evocative. Use sensory descriptions (e.g., "The weight of the wool," "The snap of the collar").
- **Constraint:** Never use "marketing speak" or over-the-top hype. Speak like a master tailor or a trusted bartender.
